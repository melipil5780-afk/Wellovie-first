What Brought You Here

This worksheet is about the story of what's been happening — what made you decide to reach out, what it's been like, and what you're carrying into this first session. There are no right answers. The more honestly you can reflect, the more useful your first session will be.

How to use this

Take 20–30 minutes with this when you're somewhere quiet. Write as much or as little as feels right. You don't have to share everything — this is your reflection first. If something is too much to write, you can note "I want to talk about this" and leave it there.

1

What's been happening

Describe, in your own words, what's been going on that led you to seek therapy now. You might talk about a specific situation, a feeling you can't shake, something that's been building, or a moment that made you decide to reach out.
