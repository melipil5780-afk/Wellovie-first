Your Steady Range

There is a zone where you function — where you can think, feel, respond, and connect. Below it you shut down. Above it you're flooded. This worksheet maps your personal range.

Date
About this worksheet

Your nervous system has three main states: flooded (too activated — anxious, panicked, reactive), steady (in your functional range — present, able to think and feel), and shutdown (too low — numb, disconnected, flat). Everyone's range and triggers are different. This worksheet is about learning yours specifically.
