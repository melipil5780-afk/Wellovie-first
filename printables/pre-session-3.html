Your Life Right Now

The thing you're bringing to therapy doesn't exist in isolation — it lives inside a whole life. This worksheet gives your therapist a picture of your world right now: what's working, what's hard, where you find support, and where things feel thin. It takes about 20 minutes.

A note on this section
