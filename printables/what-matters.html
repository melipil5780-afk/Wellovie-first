What Actually Matters

Most people are building a life toward something — but couldn't name it clearly under pressure. This worksheet surfaces the values underneath your choices so you can see whether the life you're living is oriented toward what you actually care about.

Date
What values are — and aren't

Values are not goals (things you achieve) or feelings (things you experience) — they're directions you want to move in. "I want to feel close to people" is a feeling. "Connection" is a value. There are no correct answers here, only honest ones.
