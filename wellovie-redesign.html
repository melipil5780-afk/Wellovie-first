About Wellovie Clinical

Built by a clinician.
For clinicians and clients alike.

Wellovie Clinical is part of Wellovie — a digital mental wellness platform built by a licensed mental health counselor who saw how broken the therapist discovery process was for everyone involved.

The problem we solve
Therapists are invisible.
Clients are overwhelmed.

Most therapists in private practice have no online presence beyond a bare listing on Psychology Today — if they have that. Meanwhile, clients searching for help face a fragmented, cold, and often anxiety-inducing process.

Wellovie Clinical closes that gap: a warm, human directory where practitioners can build a real presence and clients can find someone who truly fits — without giving up their privacy to do it.

Built on trust
Why we never touch
patient data.

The decision to build Wellovie Clinical as a marketing platform — not a clinical tool — was intentional and principled. By keeping all booking and patient communication in the therapist's own EHR, we protect both the client's privacy and the practitioner from unnecessary compliance burden.

This isn't a workaround. It's the right architecture. The same model used by Psychology Today, Zencare, and Headway.

Questions
Answered up front.
What happens to my website if I cancel? ▾
Your website is hosted as part of your Pro plan. Cancel and it goes offline. Your data is always yours to export. Resubscribe anytime — it comes right back.
Do I need to worry about HIPAA? ▾
No. Wellovie Clinical is a marketing platform — we never process, store, or transmit PHI. Patient communication stays in your own EHR. Zero HIPAA exposure from using our platform.
How long does the website wizard take? ▾
Under 15 minutes for most practitioners. Answer questions about your practice, specialties, and approach — we handle all design, layout, and copy.
Is there a free trial for Pro? ▾
Yes — 14 days free, no credit card required. If you love it, billing begins after the trial. If not, you drop to the free directory tier automatically.
